# Privacy Policy for Paced

**Last updated: March 6, 2026**

## Overview
Paced ("the app") is a speech fluency practice tool developed by Abbas Yousefzadeh. 
This privacy policy explains how the app handles your data.

## Data Collection
Paced does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

## Microphone Access
The app requests microphone access solely to detect speech rhythm during Live Mode 
practice sessions. Audio is processed entirely on-device in real time and is never 
recorded, stored, or transmitted anywhere.

## Data Storage
All data you enter (words and sentences for practice) is stored locally on your 
device only using standard iOS storage. It never leaves your device.

## Third-Party Services
Paced does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy
Paced does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.
